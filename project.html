Walk, Improve general Health, and Have Fun.

With Dungeon Walk, Count your steps, Track your water intake, all whilst burning calories and leading to a better you.

Health Benefits

Burn calories whilst walking.

Dungeon Walk tracks how many calories approximately you've burned by the steps you have taken.

Improve hydration by drinking water.

Here we promote water intake, allowing the user to input and track how many glasses are consumed daily.

Track how many steps you take.

Our app has a built in step tracker, allowing the user to attack and defeat monsters based off a daily step count.

Delve into the Dungeons !

Every step you take acts as an attack. Your movement in real life directly affects the dungeon mob infront of you, with each step dealing 1HP of damage!

The more active you are, the more powerful you become, turning something as simple as walking into rewarding gameplay

Other features. Quests and inventory.

Your inventory brings your adventure to life. Equip gear — from helmets and armor to weapons and rings — to boost your stats and tackle the dungeon. Earn new items through real-world effort: defeat mobs, hit step goals, drink water, and burn calories. As you grow stronger in daily life, your character becomes stronger, turning progress into rewards and making your inventory a showcase of achievements.

The quests screen tracks your daily progression, turning real-world actions into adventure. Split into Steps, Water, and Calories tabs, each offers evolving challenges — like 1,000 to 5,000 steps, drinking 5 to 10 cups of water, or burning calories through activity. Completing quests grants rewards, strengthens your character, and unlocks new dungeon opportunities, making daily habits feel like a continuous adventure.
