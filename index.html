Walk, Improve general Health, and Have Fun.

With Dungeon Walk, Count your steps, Track your water intake, all whilst burning calories and leading to a better you.

Health Benefits.

Burn calories whilst walking.

Dungeon Walk tracks how many calories approximately you've burned by the steps you have taken.

Improve hydration by drinking water.

Here we promote water intake, allowing the user to input and track how many glasses are consumed daily.

Track how many steps you take.

Our app has a built in step tracker, allowing the user to attack and defeat monsters based off a daily step count.

Delve into the Dungeons !

Every step you take acts as an attack. Your movement in real life directly affects the dungeon mob infront of you, with each step dealing 1HP of damage!

The more active you are, the more powerful you become, turning something as simple as walking into rewarding gameplay

Other features. Inventory.

Your inventory is what brings your adventure to life. Use items ranging from armour like chest-pieces and helmets to weapons and rings to improve your damage and resistance when exploring the dungeon! Acquire new items through gameplay: defeating enemies, reaching your step goals, drinking water, and burning calories. When you improve your life, you improve your characters also, and your inventory becomes a reflection of your hard-work!

Quests

The Quests tab turns everyday activities into a fun adventure. The Quests screen is divided into three categories, steps, water, and calories, with respective challenges that reset daily. For instance, taking steps ranging from 1,000 to 5,000 steps a day, drinking water from 5 to 10 cups a day or burning a set number of calories a day. Successfully fulfilling these Quests rewards the user with character XP, and new pieces of armour. Accessing the Quests tab turns daily habits into a fun and a constant process.
